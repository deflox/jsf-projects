What is reecepy?

With reecepy you can plan your food plan on a two week basis. For that you can get together in a circle.

What is a circle?

A circle is what we call a family. When you sign up for our service, you will not be in any circle. Well at least you are not in one with other members. You are in your own circle as default. Now you are able to invite every user by his email, if he is available for an invite. It is also possible to just use reecepy for yourself as you can turn off your availbility to get invited by disabled the ticked box in preferences under circles.

All members in a circle have the same rights. They can remove and add other members to the circle and shop stuff with the shopping list tool. And of course they can vote for different suggestion and add new ones for the next two weeks.

How can I change my profile icon?

We are managing your profile icon with an external service called gravatar. With gravatar, you have all your profile images at one place. To get your own icon, just create an account at gravatar and add one or more of your emails to gravatar and assign it to an uploaded profile image.

Importan: The email adress you are use in gravatar for the image assigment must be the same email as here. So you might wanna change it at the preferences view. If the emails are the same, your image will be displayed here.

Can I add recipes on my own?

Currently this isn't possible to handle. We are trying to offer you a wide variation of recipes, so that you never miss one for your plan. If you still think, that there is one, which is missing write an email at contact@reecepy.com to propose your idea. We are open for any suggestions!

What is the shopping list for?

Our shopping list is a useful tool to create your shopping list with your suggestions. By default every best voted recipe is already checked. But you can check other to recipes too to add it to your shopping list.

And it's even possible to add additional items to your list, so that you can use this shopping list for other things too!
