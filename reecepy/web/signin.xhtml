What is reecepy?

Are you stuck at planing your weekly food plan? Does your family complain about the food you serve, because they wanted something different?

Fuck these problems and plan everything with just a few clicks! Save time and enjoy cooking again, cause everyone will be happy with what you serve.

Features

  • - Add recipes on a daily basis
  • - Vote as a circle member for recipes
  • - Manage your circles
  • - View detail info about one recipe
  • - Generate a shopping list based on your selected recipes
